# Acme Widgets, Inc. — Privacy Policy (sample)

> This is a deliberately incomplete sample policy used by the toolkit's
> `analyze_policy` tool to demonstrate gap analysis. It is NOT legal advice
> and should not be used as a template for any real policy.

## What we collect

We collect names, email addresses, and shipping addresses when you create an
account or place an order. We also collect payment-card information through
our payment processor; we do not store full card numbers on our servers.
Browsing activity on our site is logged for security and analytics.

## How we use it

We use your data to fulfil orders, send transactional emails (order
confirmations, shipping notifications), and -- with your consent -- send
marketing emails about new products. You can withdraw marketing consent at
any time using the unsubscribe link in any marketing email.

## Sharing

We share data with our payment processor, our shipping carrier, and our
email marketing platform. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

## Retention

We keep account data for as long as your account is active.

## Your rights

You can access or correct your account data by logging into your account
settings. For other requests, contact privacy@acmewidgets.example.

## Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@acmewidgets.example.
